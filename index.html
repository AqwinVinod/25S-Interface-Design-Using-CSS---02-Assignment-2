Lisbon in 48 Hours: What Not to Miss

July 27, 2025 by Aqwin and Alphin

From pastel streets to seaside viewpoints, Lisbon is a traveler’s dream. Here’s how I explored Portugal’s capital in just two days — with must-see highlights and hidden finds.

What Made It Special

Walking through Alfama’s cobbled lanes felt like stepping into a painting. The smell of grilled sardines and the sounds of Fado music made it unforgettable.

Quick Tips

  • Ride Tram 28 early to avoid crowds
  • Visit Time Out Market for food variety
  • Wear comfy shoes — Lisbon is hilly!
